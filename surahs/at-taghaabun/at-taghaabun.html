Mindmap for Surah at-Taghabun

mindmap root((Soorat at-Taghabun)) Allah's Attributes and Power (1: All in the heavens and earth glorify Allah) (1: Allah is the All-Mighty, Most Wise) (2: Allah created all, including the believers and disbelievers) (3: Allah created and shaped you, and to Him is the return) (4: Allah knows all, hidden and open) (13: Allah is All-Knowing and All-Wise) The Deception of the World and Testing (14-16: Your wealth and your children are a trial) (15-16: The need to put your trust in Allah alone) (16: The need to fear Allah and to be pious) Guidance and Warnings (10: If you lend a goodly loan to Allah, he will multiply it for you) (12-13: Obey Allah and His Messenger, Allah knows all that is in the hearts) (13-14: Allah does not burden the believers) The Reward and Punishment (10: The reward for giving is forgiveness and a good recompense) (11: Those who turn away will face a painful punishment) (16-18: The need for patience and for Allah to see and know) (18: Allah is forgiving and merciful to those who return to Him)
