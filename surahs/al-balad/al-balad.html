Mindmap for Surah Surah Al-Balad

mindmap root((Soorat al-Balad)) Divine Oaths and Significance (1-3: Oaths by Makkah and the creation of Adam and his progeny) Human Existence and Hardship (4: Mankind was created to endure hardship) (5: Thinking that no one can over power them is a mistake) (6-7: The love of wealth and boasting leads people astray) The Paths of Goodness and Righteousness (11-16: Man is given a choice to follow the difficult path which is that of good conduct and seeking what is right) (12-16: The path of doing good deeds such as freeing slaves, and feeding the needy) Those Who Follow Evil (13-16: They have followed evil because of love of wealth and their arrogance) The Promise of Divine Recompense and Justice (17-20: Those who are righteous will attain great success) (17-20: Those who follow the path of evil will be punished)
