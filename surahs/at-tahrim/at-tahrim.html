Mindmap for Surah at-Tahreem

mindmap root((Soorat at-Tahreem)) Divine Guidance and Prohibition (1-5: The Prophet is rebuked for prohibiting what was lawful for him) (2: Allah has prescribed the way of releasing yourselves from oaths) (4: Sincere repentance is the way to avoid divine wrath) (6: Protect yourselves and your families from the fire) Prophet's Household (3-5: How Allah supported the Prophet and His wives and warns about their behaviour) (6-10: The necessity of following the Messenger) (8-9: The importance of sincere repentance and a good outcome) (11-12: Examples of women in the past and their submission to Allah) Allah's Attributes and Power (2: Allah is All-Knowing, Most Wise, and your guardian) (6: Angels carry out Allah's command) The Need to Strive and Seek Guidance (4: To have faith and support Allah and His Messenger) (7-10: The need to fear Allah and to obey the messengers of Allah) (9: The need to repent and seek Allah's forgiveness) (10: The believers should be aware of the deception of Shaytan)
