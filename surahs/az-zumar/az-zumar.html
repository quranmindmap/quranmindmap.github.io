Key Themes

graph LR A[Surah az-Zumar] --> B(Verses 1-9: Divine Origin of the Qur'an and Sincere Devotion to Allah); A --> C(Verses 10-21: Exhortation to Worship Allah, and the different status of those who do and those who do not); A --> D(Verses 22-31: The state of those who are guided and those who are astray from the truth); A --> E(Verses 32-54: Inevitability of Judgement and consequences of falsehood, mercy towards the believers); A --> F(Verses 55-75: Call to Repentance, Hope in Divine Forgiveness and the Promise of Paradise and Hell); B --> B1(The Qur'an revealed from Allah, the Wise and All-Knowing); B --> B2(Sincere Devotion is due to Allah alone); C --> C1(Devotion to Allah alone leads to Guidance and Success); C --> C2(Misguidance and its Consequences); D --> D1(The condition of the guided and their hearts in purity); D --> D2(The condition of the unguided and their hearts in disbelief); E --> E1(Allah’s power over all and all will return to Him); E --> E2(Those who ascribe partners to Allah will have no helpers or intercessors); F --> F1(Allah’s command to turn to Him and seek His mercy); F --> F2(The reward of the believers and punishment of the disbelievers);
