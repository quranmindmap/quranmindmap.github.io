Mindmap for Surah Nuh

mindmap root((Soorat Nooh)) The Mission and Message of Nooh (1-4: Nooh was sent as a clear warner to his people) (3: He called them to worship Allah and obey him) The Rejection of His People (5-6: Nooh's people persisted in their disbelief) (7: They reject his call) (7: They try to avoid hearing the message) Nooh's Supplication and Allah's Response (26: The nature of the disbelievers that made Nooh pray to Allah to destroy them) (27: If they are left to live, they will do nothing but mislead) (28: A Supplication to Allah for forgiveness) Allah's Power and Mercy (10-12: Allah’s abundant blessings and promises for those who believe) (17-20: Allah is the One Who created mankind and the earth and can bring about the resurrection) The Consequences of Disbelief (25: Those who disbelieved will be punished) (27: The disbelievers will only produce evil offspring) The Importance of Divine Revelation (1-4: Allah sent Nooh as a clear warner) (21-24: Nooh is truthful)
