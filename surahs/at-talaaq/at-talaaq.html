Mindmap for Surah At-Talaaq

mindmap root((Soorat at-Talaq)) Rules for Divorce (1: Divorce should occur at the proper time) (1: An accurate count of the waiting period must be kept) (1: Fear Allah when issuing divorce) (1: Do not turn women out of their homes during waiting period) (1-2: Maintain women and avoid disputes) Observing the Rights of the Wives (2: Retain wives on equitable terms or part with them on equitable terms) (2: Two just witnesses should be called during divorce or reconciliation) (4: The waiting period of those who have ceased menstruating is three months) (4: The waiting period of the pregnant is until they give birth) (6-7: Accommodate women according to means and provide for them) The Importance of Righteousness and Submission to Allah (2: To give honest testimony for Allah) (2: When one fears Allah, He will find a way out of difficulty) (3: Allah will provide from where one does not expect) (3: If you fear Allah, you will be blessed with ease in your matters) (4: To act for the sake of Allah, and you will be rewarded) Allah's Power and Control (3: Allah’s will and power are all encompassing) (12: Allah is the One who created the heavens and earth and sent down his decrees) The Consequences of Disobedience (1: Transgressing Allah’s limits will lead to wrongdoing) (4: Disbelievers will have a painful punishment) (12: All will be judged on the basis of Allah’s decree)
