Mindmap for Surah al-muddathir

mindmap root((Soorat al-Muddaththir)) The Prophet's Commission (1-2: The Prophet is addressed as one who is covered in garments, and is commanded to arise and warn) (3: Allah is the One who must be magnified) (4: To purify your garments) (5: Avoid all forms of abomination) (6: Do not give with the aim of gaining something in return) (7: Have patience for the sake of Allah) The Rejection and Warning (11-26: The description of a stubborn rejector, his traits, and his horrible end) (26-30: The detailed description of what will happen to the disbelievers) (32-37: Allah has set the limits and there is a warning to mankind to take heed) (48: The disbelievers who refuse the guidance of Allah are to be warned, and if not they will be punished.) Allah's Decree and Power (31: Allah's control over people) (36: Allah is most worthy of being feared) The Qur'an and its Purpose (31: A reminder to those who want to pay heed) (54-56: The Qur’an is a reminder to all people and it is a clear warning) The Importance of the Hereafter (37: The importance of remembering the hereafter) (38-40: Description of those in paradise and those in hell) Divine Wisdom and Attributes (41: Allah is the Possessor of abundant grace, and is the source of guidance) (43-56: The description of the wicked and the disbelievers and their fate)
