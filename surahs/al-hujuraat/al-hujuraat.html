Mindmap for Surah al-Hujurat

mindmap root((Soorat al-Hujurât)) Adab -Manners- in the presence of Allah and Messenger (1-3: Do not proceed with matters before Allah and His Messenger) (2: Do not raise your voices above the Prophet or speak loudly to him) Adab Among Believers (6: Verify information, lest you harm innocents) (11: Do not ridicule or give bad nicknames) (12: Avoid suspicion, spying and backbiting) (13: Mankind is one family and should know one another) (10: Make peace between fighting brothers) (10: Believers are but brothers, and the importance of their relationship) The Superiority of the Righteous (13: Nobility is based on righteousness, not family lineage) (15-18: Allah knows best those who have true faith, and they are to submit to Him) Guidance on Faith and Conduct (6: If a wrongdoer brings you news, verify it) (11: Do not attribute faults to your brothers) (12: Shun suspicion and backbiting) (13: Know the different tribes so that people may recognise one another) Divine Knowledge (6: Allah is All-Hearing, All-Seeing) (13: Allah is All-Knowing, All-Aware) Allah's Authority and Power (18: Allah has knowledge of all things) (18: Allah’s decree cannot be altered)
