Mindmap for Surah Al-Fajr

mindmap root((Soorat al-Fajr)) Divine Oaths and Their Significance (1-4: Oaths by the dawn, ten nights, the even and the odd, and the night when it departs) The Fate of Past Nations and the Reminder of Punishment (5-14: What Allah did to 'Ad, Thamood, and Pharaoh) (12: Allah has full knowledge of His creation and all their deeds) The Nature of Man (15-20: Man’s actions and his love of worldly things) (15-16: Man is tested by Allah with provision, whether it is abundant or restricted) (17-18: The need to show gratitude to Allah) (20: Man’s Love of Wealth and Greed) (21: The realities of the Day of Reckoning will become clear) The Recompense on the Day of Judgment (21-26: Description of the doom of hell and the punishment of the disbelievers) (27-30: The rewards of the righteous in paradise)
