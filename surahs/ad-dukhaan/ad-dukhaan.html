Mindmap for Surah Ad-Dukhan

mindmap root((Soorat ad-Dukhan)) Divine Revelation and Warning (1-6: The Qur'an is a clear Book sent down during a blessed night) (3: Allah sends Messengers as a mercy) (3: The Qur'an as a warning) (4-5: All matters are decided on the basis of wisdom and by divine command) Rejection and Their Fate (7-9: The rejection of the disbelievers) (10-16: Warning of the coming of a visible smoke as a sign) (17-29: The arrogance of Pharaoh and his people, and their destruction) (30-33: Allah's destruction of Pharaoh and his people) (34-37: Allah's power and warnings) (35: Those who argue against Our signs will know that they have no escape.) The Nature of Man (49-51: Man's lack of patience in ease and hardship) (51: Man resorts to supplication during hardship) Allah's Attributes and Power (4: Allah is the Most High, the Most Great) (5: Allah is Oft-Forgiving, Most Merciful) (11: Allah sends down rain and gives life to the dead) Divine Judgement (25, 52-54: Allah will punish the wrongdoers) (52: Allah will requite each person for their deeds) (53: Allah will show His signs until it becomes clear to them that Qur'an is true) (54: Allah encompasses all things)
