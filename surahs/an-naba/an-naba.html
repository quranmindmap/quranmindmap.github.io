Mindmap for Surah an-Naba

mindmap root((Soorat an-Naba')) The Great News and Questioning (1-3: What are they asking each other about?) (1-3: About the great tidings, about which they differ.) Allah's Power and Signs in Creation (6-16: The earth, mountains, and their benefits) (7: The mountains are like pegs holding the earth) (8: Creation of mankind in pairs) (9: The need for sleep and night as a cover) (10-11: The day as a means of livelihood) (12: The seven firmaments) (13: A blazing lamp) (14-16: The source of rain which brings forth crops) The Day of Judgement and Its Realities (17-18: The Day of Judgement has a specific time) (18-19: The blast on the trumpet and the gathering of people) (19: Description of the heaven being opened) (20: The description of the mountains) The Destinies of the Righteous and the Transgressors (21-30: Descriptions of hell and the torment therein, and the fate of transgressors) (31-36: Descriptions of paradise, and the blessings of the righteous, as well as a promise of triumph) The warnings and exhortations (37: It is not proper for the believers to put on the attributes of the arrogant, it is also for Allah’s slaves to learn of the great status of Allah) (38: The day of reckoning will be established when all souls stand before Allah) (38: All creation will be silent on the Day of Judgment) (39-40: Allah's warnings to those who deny the truth, about the severity of the consequences)
