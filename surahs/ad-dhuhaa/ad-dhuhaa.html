Mindmap for Surah Ad-Dhuhaa

mindmap root((Soorat ad-Duhâ)) Divine Oaths and their Significance (1-2: Allah swears by the day and night) Allah's Favor to His Messenger (3-5: Allah has not forsaken his Messenger nor does He hate him; rather Allah gives him an eternal reward) The Blessings Bestowed Upon The Prophet (6: He was an orphan whom Allah sheltered and protected) (7: He was unaware of the true path which Allah then guided him to) (8: He was in poverty, so Allah enriched him) The Importance of Guidance and Gratitude (9-10: The need to be kind and merciful) (9-10: Do not turn away the orphan or the beggar) (11: Speak of Allah’s blessings)
