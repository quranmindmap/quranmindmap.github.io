Mindmap for Surah Ad-dhariyat

mindmap root((Soorat adh-Dhariyat)) Divine Oaths and Promises (1-6: The oaths of the wind, clouds, stars, and angels) (7-9: the truth of Allah's promises) Actions and Their Consequences (10-14: The fate of those who base their beliefs on conjecture) (15-19: The characteristics and reward of the righteous) (20-23: The clear signs of Allah in the earth and their own selves) (24-30: The disbelievers will be requited on the Day of Judgement) (31-37: Characteristics of the Believers and their reward in Paradise) (41-42: Allah’s power) (43-45: Allah’s power over disbelievers and their punishment) Divine Attributes and Power (47-51: The blessings and provisions Allah bestows upon His creation) (52-53: Allah will show His signs until it becomes clear to them that Qur'an is the truth) (54-55: How the righteous fear Allah) (56-58: How the disbelievers reject Allah) (59-60: The message is a reminder) Divine Will and Purpose (56: Humans are created only to worship Allah alone) (57-60: Allah is the sole source of provision, and He has complete control over His creation)
