Mindmap for Surah al-ahqaf

mindmap root((Soorat al-Ahqaf)) Divine Revelation and Truth (1-3: The Qur'an is a revelation from Allah) (1-3: The Qur'an is with truth and for a true purpose) Disbelievers' Rejection and Consequences (3: The disbelievers are heedless of Allah's warnings) (4-6: Polytheists are misguided and take gods besides Allah) (7-10: The disbelievers reject the message, yet they cannot produce anything like the Qur'an) (11-12: Disbelievers claim they only follow what they found their forefathers upon) (20: The disbeliever’s punishment in the hereafter) (20: The Disbelievers do not fear the Hereafter) Affirmation of Allah’s Attributes and Power (4-6: Allah’s control of the heavens and the earth) (7-10: Allah’s power and might are above all else) (20: Allah is the owner of the blessings of this world) Guidance and Divine Path (13-14: To follow the right path) (15-16: The Qur'an must be followed because it came from Allah) (17-19: Those who turn to the truth will be guided) Accountability (17-19: The disbelievers' arrogance and ultimate outcome) (20: The people of falsehood have no way to save themselves) Importance of Reflection and Admonition (21-26: The need to pay attention to the Qur'an and follow its instructions) (21-26: The believers followed the message and they will be rewarded) (27-28: Disbelievers are given warning in the form of the story of 'Ad and their destruction) (34-35: Be patient and grateful)
