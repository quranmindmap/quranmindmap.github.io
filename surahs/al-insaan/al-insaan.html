Mindmap for Surah al-insan

mindmap root((Soorat al-Insân)) The Creation of Man (1-3: Creation of man from a mingled drop of fluid) (2-4: Allah bestows upon man hearing, sight and sent Messengers to show the way) The Two Paths and Their Outcomes (3: Man can choose to be grateful or ungrateful) (5: The disbelievers are those who are in the blazing fire) (4-10: Description of Paradise and its blessings) The Nature of the Righteous (5-10: The righteous will drink from a cup mixed with camphor) (11-22: Description of the righteous and how they have acted) Divine Blessings and Guidance (13: The people of Paradise will be reclining on couches and will have many blessings) (14-21: The people of paradise are promised many blessings) The Rewards of the Righteous (19-20: Servants will circle them, and they will see great beauty in Paradise) (11-21: They will receive a reward from Allah, which includes bliss and delight)
