Mindmap for Surah Al-Lail

mindmap root((Soorat al-Layl)) Divine Oaths and Their Significance (1-4: Oaths by the night and the day, and the creation of male and female) The Two Paths and Their Outcomes (5-7: Those who give in charity, fear Allah, and affirm the ultimate good, will have an easy path to salvation) (8-11: Those who are miserly, think themselves self-sufficient, and reject the ultimate good will have an easy path to perdition, and their wealth will not save them) The Divine Guidance (12: Allah is the provider of all guidance) (13: The hereafter and this world belong to Allah) The Consequences of Rejection (14-16: The fire of Hell is a warning for those who deny the truth) (17-21: Those who give for the sake of Allah and who are sincere will find blessings and will be well pleased)
