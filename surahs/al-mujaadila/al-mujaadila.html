Mindmap for Surah al-mujaadila

mindmap root((Soorat al-Mujadilah)) Allah's Awareness and Response (1: Allah hears and is aware of all complaints and discussions) (4: Allah knows all that is done) The Error of Dhihar (2: The error of likening one’s wife to their mothers) (3-4: How to retract from Dhihar; expiation) (5: The laws of Islam are those of Allah) The importance of proper conduct (2: Proper etiquette when speaking to the Messenger) (5: Allah guides those who seek his pleasure) (6: The need to be just and truthful in all dealings) (7: People should not engage in private conversation that is for bad intentions) (7: Believers must trust in Allah for His help and protection) The Hypocrites' Nature (1-6: The behavior and intention of the hypocrites) (14-15: The hypocrites do not fear Allah) (16: They are liars, and are only deceiving themselves) (22: They belong to the party of Satan, they will be the losers in the hereafter) The Believers (9: The believers must strive for piety and righteousness) (11: Allah raises the status of those who believe and have knowledge) (18-19: The believers trust in Allah, and love those whom Allah loves) Divine Justice (4-5: Disbelievers will face severe punishment) (16: Those who oppose the truth will not be able to oppose Allah, as He is All-Mighty and All-Powerful) (20: The punishment of those who oppose Allah and His Messenger is assured) (21: The believers will be of great success)
