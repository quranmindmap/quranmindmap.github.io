Mindmap for Surah Ash-Shura

mindmap root((Soorat ash-Shoora)) Introduction, Divine Attributes, and Revelation (1-3: The Qur'an as Revelation) (4-6: Allah as the Most High, the Most Great, and All-Knowing) (5: Angels glorifying Allah and seeking forgiveness for believers) (6: Polytheists taking protectors besides Allah) (3: The Messenger’s message is similar to previous messengers) (7: The message was revealed in Arabic to warn Makkah and surrounding areas) Allah's Power and Control (9: Allah is the only Protector) (9: Allah is the controller of life and death) (9: Allah has power over all things) (12: Allah has control over blessings and provision) Human Accountability and Response (49: Human's impaitence with ease and hardship) (50: Human's ingratitude with blessings) (49-51: The righteous are patient and grateful) (52-54: The Qur'an is for all and one must understand and comply to it) (16: Those who argue against the Qur'an will be punished) The Day of Judgement (7: The Day of Gathering) (7-9: Dividing of people into two groups in Paradise and Hell) (54: Those who reject the Qur'an will be outwitted by Allah) The Need for Submission and Unity (13: Obey Allah and his Messenger and his commandments) (13: Uphold religion and do not become divided) (10: Seek Allah’s guidance and turn to Him) (15: Do not argue after clear guidance has come) The Importance of Balance and Equity (15: Allah requires that people should be treated justly) (10: Allah is just in all his decisions) (11: Allah is all-hearing and all-seeing)
