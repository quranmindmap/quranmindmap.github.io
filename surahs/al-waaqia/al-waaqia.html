Mindmap for Surah a-waqiah

mindmap root((Soorat al-Waqi'ah)) The Inevitable Event and Its Impact (1-6: The Inevitable Event will occur, bringing some low and raising others) The Three Groups (7: People will be divided into three kinds) (8-10: Those on the Right will be blessed) (41-56: The People of the Left will be miserable) (11-14: The Foremost in Faith will be closest to Allah) Descriptions of Paradise and Hell (15-26: Description of the pleasures of Paradise) (27-34: Description of the blessed who are given glad tidings) (35-56: Description of the blessed and their companions and how they will be honoured) (41-56: Descriptions of the hellfire and its torment) The Reminder and Reflection (57-70: Allah has created everything, and has control over life and death) (71-82: Allah provided everything for people to use and benefit from in this life) (83-87: Those in the throes of death will be helpless) (88-96: The fate of those who are near to Allah and those who reject him)
