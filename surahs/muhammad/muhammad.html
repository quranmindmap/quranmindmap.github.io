Mindmap for Surah Muhammad

mindmap root((Soorat Muhammad)) The Believers (1-2: Allah will guide and rectify those who believe and do good deeds) (4-7: The qualities and benefits of those who strive in Allah's cause) (20-23: Believers should not let their good deeds go to waste and should persist) The Disbelievers (1-3: Allah renders the deeds of disbelievers invalid and leads them to destruction) (17-18: Those who disbelieve are far from divine guidance, and how it leads them astray) (20-23: Their disbelief leads to an increase in their evil actions and their bad outcome) The Nature of the Struggle (4-7: The Muslims will prevail, but need to be steadfast) (8-12: The believers must support the cause of Allah and His Messenger) (30-32: Disbelievers are recognized by the diseases of their hearts) (36-38: The reward is in the hereafter and not in this world, so seek that) The Qur'an as the Truth (1-3: The Qur'an is the truth from Allah) (14: The Qur'an contains guidance that is needed by those who want to strive) Divine Justice and Divine Support (4: Allah is the All Knowing, All Wise) (4: Allah tests people so they will become known for their good or evil) (5, 15: Allah will make the believers steadfast and support them in their efforts) (14: Allah guides those who believe and who turn to Him for that guidance) (38: Allah is All-Seeing, and all things will return to Him) (4-7: Allah has ultimate power and will) (35: Allah will not let the believers lose out on their deeds) (38: Allah will never detract from the reward of the believers)
