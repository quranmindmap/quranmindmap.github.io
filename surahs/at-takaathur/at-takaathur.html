Mindmap for Surah At-Takaathur

mindmap root((Soorat at-Takathur)) Distraction and Competition (1-2: Competition for worldly gain distracts from the remembrance of Allah until death) Warning and Reminder (3-4: People will come to know what they will face) (5: If people knew the truth with certainty, they would not be distracted by the world) (6-7: You will surely see hell and what is in store there) (8: People will be questioned about the blessings of Allah in this world)
