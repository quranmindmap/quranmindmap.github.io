Mindmap for Surah an-naaziaat

mindmap root((Soorat an-Nazi'at)) Divine Oaths and Their Significance (1-5: Oaths sworn by the angels who extract souls, and carry out divine commands) The Day of Resurrection (6-9: The events that will occur on that day) (6: The earth will be shaken and the first blast of the Trumpet) (7-8: It will be followed by the second blast, and hearts will be pounding) The Fate of the Disbelievers (10-14: The disbelievers will reject the resurrection, and ask when the hour will occur) (21-23: Pharaoh rejected the signs of Moosa and disobeyed Allah) (25-26: Allah seizes the disbelievers for their sin, for he is the most strong and powerful) The Story of Moosa (15-20: The story of Moosa being called to prophethood and shown great signs) (17-21: Moosa was sent to pharaoh to enjoin belief in Allah and good conduct) Divine Power and Might (27-33: Allah has created the universe, the earth, and all life, and sustains it) (27-33: Allah controls all creation) The Inevitability of Reckoning (27-37: Allah has the power to recreate mankind, and all deeds will be made apparent) (34-37: Those who deny the Truth will experience their punishment on that day) (38-41: Those who reject guidance will be punished severely) (38-41: They will face punishment for having preferred this life and for rejecting truth)
