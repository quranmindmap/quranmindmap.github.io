Mindmap for Surah Al-Maa'un

mindmap root((Soorat al-Ma'oon)) Denial of the Hereafter (1: Have you seen the one who denies the Last Judgement?) The Rejection of the Needy (2: He drives away the orphan) (3: He does not urge others to feed the needy) The Insincerity of their Prayer (4-5: Woe to those who pray, but are heedless regarding their prayer) False Piety and Withholding (6: Those who make a show of piety) (7: Those who withhold even the smallest kindnesses)
