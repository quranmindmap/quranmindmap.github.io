Mindmap for Surah Az-Zukhruf

mindmap root((Soorat az-Zukhruf)) Divine Revelation and its Purpose (1-5: The Qur'an is a clear Book in Arabic, from the Mother of the Book) (3: The purpose is so you may understand) (3: Allah is All-Wise and All-Mighty) (4-5: The Qur'an is with Allah and is sublime and full of wisdom) Rejection and Arrogance (13: Allah's warning to polytheists about the hardness of his message) (14: The polytheists differ only out of envy after having received knowledge) (15-25: Polytheists' rejection of the truth, they have no proof or rational basis) (43: The punishment for the disbelievers is severe) (52: What do you think, if it is from Allah, but you reject it?) (74-78: Allah will cause the disbelievers to taste a harsh punishment) Allah's Attributes and Power (4: Allah is the Most High and the Most Great, and His is all that is in the heavens and earth) (12: Allah controls the heavens and the earth and bestows provision) (13: Allah guides those who turn to Him) (13: Allah chooses whomever He wills) Prophethood and Guidance (13: Allah has prescribed the same religion for all Messengers) (13: They are to uphold the religion and not be divided) (15: The Prophet is to call people to his religion) Accountability (15: Allah will bring them together on the Day of Judgement) (16: Those who argue against the truth will be punished) (52: You will know if you are right or wrong when Allah judges) Signs of Allah (53: Allah will show them signs in the universe and in their own selves) (53: The testimony of Allah is sufficient and that the Qur'an is the truth) (54: Allah encompasses all things)
