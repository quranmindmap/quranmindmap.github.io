Mindmap for Surah al-hashr

mindmap root((Soorat al-Hashr)) Divine Power and Control (1: All in the heavens and earth glorifies Allah, for He has power over all) (5: Allah has power over all things and that all is under His control) (6: Allah bestows what He wills) The Expulsion of Banu an-Nadir (2: Allah drove out the disbelievers, though they thought they could resist) (3: Allah decreed exile upon them) (4-5: Those who oppose Allah face severe consequences) (5: Allah has power over the believers and their enemies) The Source of Authority and Guidance (7: How to allocate the spoils of war) (9: How to attain true guidance, and the need to seek forgiveness for sins) (10: Supplication to Allah to avoid all grudges) The Believers (8: They seek grace from Allah, support Allah's cause and His Messenger) (9: They are generous to their brothers and give preference to others) The Attributes of Allah (1-4: Allah’s attributes of perfection and knowledge) (22-24: Allah is worthy of all veneration and that His beautiful names, attributes and actions are a foundation of belief) The Quran as Reminder and Guidance (21: The Qur'an is divine revelation and it has power) (21: If Allah willed, He could bring forth all His power and destroy all) (22-24: The Qur'an is a reminder for all people to reflect, learn and be guided)
