Mindmap for Surah al-ma'arij

mindmap root((Soorat al-Ma'arij)) Divine Punishment and Inevitability (1-3: A Sceptic asks about punishment and Allah controls the routes of ascent) (4: The angels ascend to Allah in a day whose measure is fifty thousand years) (7: The disbelievers regard the punishment as far away, but it is close to Allah) (13-14: Allah knows the secrets and private gatherings) (15-18: On the day of Judgement, the hypocrites will be distressed and wish to ransom themselves) Man's Nature and Conduct (19-21: The nature of man is to be fretful and impatient) (21: The believers are aware of the great reward of the hereafter and seek guidance) (22-35: They are steadfast in prayer and are always on guard of their duties and obligations) The Attributes of the Righteous (24: The righteous have a prescribed share for the needy in their wealth) (25: They have a clear understanding of faith and the hereafter) (27-31: The people of truth have a deep fear and respect for Allah and strive to be pleasing to Him) Divine Power and Control (4: Allah has control over everything and His is all that ascends to Him) (30-31: Allah has power over destiny) (38-40: All deeds are recorded in the books of records) Warnings and Reminders (1-3: Allah is the One from whom is the divine punishment) (3: The righteous seek closeness to Allah through His attributes and name) (5-7: Be patient with your calling and worship, for Allah’s promise is true) (36-41: The disbelievers will not believe until they are punished) (42-44: Allah’s power will bring all to account)
