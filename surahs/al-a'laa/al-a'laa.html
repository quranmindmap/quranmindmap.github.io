Mindmap for Surah al-a'laa

mindmap root((Soorat al-A'lâ)) Glorifying Allah (1: Glorify the name of your Lord, the Most High) Divine Creation and Guidance (2-3: Allah's Creation and predetermination) (4-5: Allah provides pasture) The Divine Revelation (6-7: The revelation and memorisation of the Qur'an) (8: Allah will ease the path for you to attain paradise) The Reminder and its Benefit (9-10: Exhortation will only benefit those who fear Allah) (11-13: Those who reject the message will enter hell and will not die in it nor live) (14-15: The success of those who purify themselves) (16-17: The preference of the disbelievers for the worldly life over the hereafter) (18-19: This message is also present in the previous scriptures)
