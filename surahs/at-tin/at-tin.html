Mindmap for Surah At-Tin

mindmap root((Soorat at-Teen)) Divine Oaths and Creation (1-3: Oaths sworn by the Fig, the Olive, Mount Sinai and Makkah) (4: Allah created mankind in the best of conditions) Human Destiny and Choices (5: Then man is reduced to the lowest of the low ) (6: Those who believe and do righteous deeds will have a reward that will never cease) Divine Questioning and Authority (7-8: What made you deny the Judgement when Allah is the Wisest of the wise)
