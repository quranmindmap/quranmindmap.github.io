Mindmap for Surah al-muzzamil

mindmap root((Soorat al-Muzzammil)) The Command to Pray at Night (1-4: The command to stand up in prayer during the night) (4: To recite the Qur’an in the night) (6: To pray at night for proper focus and recitation) The Burden of Revelation (5: The immense weight of the Qur’an) (8: Devote yourself completely to Allah) (7: Allah provides time during the day to deal with worldly affairs) The Need for Patience and Reliance (10: To bear patiently with the opposition) (9: To have reliance on Allah alone) Patience and Persistence in Da'wah (10: To keep your distance from the disbelievers) (10: Remember and Glorify Allah) Following the Prophetic Example (8: Constantly remember the name of your Lord) (7: To live a life of worship and devotion)
