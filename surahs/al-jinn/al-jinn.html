Mindmap for Surah al-Jinn

mindmap root((Soorat al-Jinn)) The Jinn's Experience with the Qur'an (1-2: A group of jinn listened to the Qur'an and affirmed its guidance) The Oneness of Allah and Rejection of Polytheism (2: The Jinn affirmed their belief and divine oneness) (3-5: Allah has no wife or children, and is far above what they attribute to Him) The Human Condition (6: Some humans seek refuge with the jinn, increasing them in transgression) (7: The disbelievers among humans think that Allah would not send Messengers) (14: There are both righteous and unrighteous people among the jinn) Divine Revelation and Guidance (1-2: Allah revealed to the Prophet that a group of jinn had heard the Qur'an) (16: The importance of adhering to the guidance) (17: Those who adhere to guidance will be rewarded, and those who turn away will be punished) Allah's Power and Knowledge (26-28: Allah is the Knower of the Unseen, and protects his Messengers) (20: All are subservient to Allah's will) The Messengers' Duty (20-23: The messengers are to convey Allah's message) (23: The duty to submit to Allah and His Messengers )
