Mindmap for Surah Abasa

mindmap root((Soorat 'Abasa)) The Prophet's Conduct and Divine Rebuke (1-10: Allah rebukes the Prophet for turning away from the blind man for the sake of the rich) The Nature of Guidance (4: The importance of the Qur'an) (5-10: The importance of proper heed and seeking guidance) The Choice Before Humanity (11-16: The nature of those who seek knowledge and guidance, and the fate of those who turn away) (17-32: Descriptions of Allah’s creation and the provisions for humankind) (33-42: The events of the Day of Judgment and each persons ultimate destination) The Aftermath of Judgement (34-37: Families will be separated on that day; each will be concerned only with their own state) (42: The different fates that await mankind on the Day of Judgement)
