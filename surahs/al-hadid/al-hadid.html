Mindmap for Surah

mindmap root((Soorat al-Hadeed)) Allah's Attributes and Power (1-3: Allah’s attributes of being first, last, manifest, hidden and all-knowing, and His power over all) (3: Allah’s creation and dominion of all things) (4: Allah’s all-encompassing knowledge and control) (16-17: Allah’s control of hearts and their actions) The Purpose of this World and the Hereafter (4: To return all matters back to Allah) (20: Life is a fleeting vanity and deception) (21: The need to race towards Paradise) (22-23: Events only happen by Allah's decree and people should not grieve or exult over them) Belief, Piety, and Spending in Allah's Cause (7: The duty to believe in Allah and his Messengers, and to spend in charity) (10-11: Allah's reward to the believers who give in charity and strive for His pleasure) (16-17: Allah’s warning about the hearts of the disbelievers being hardened by the world) (18-20: The need to give charity and good deeds with humility) (22-24: Reassurance that all affairs are under divine decree) Consequences of Disbelief and Hypocrisy (14-15: The Hypocrites are separated from the believers) (14-15: Hypocrites are in great distress on the Day of Judgement and will have no light) (16: Allah knows all, including the evil intentions of the disbelievers) (20: The life of this world is fleeting and deceiving) (22-24: Disbelievers are reprimanded and will be punished for their actions) The Importance of Humility and Reliance on Allah (18-20: Those who give must do so with humility, and put their trust in Allah) (21-24: The Believers must strive for righteousness and must believe in Allah and be patient)
