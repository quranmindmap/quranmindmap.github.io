Mindmap for Surah Al-jaathiya

mindmap root((Soorat al-Jathiyah)) Introduction and Divine Signs (1-4: Revelation of the Book from Allah, the All-Mighty, the Most Wise) (3-5: The Signs of Allah in the Heavens, the Earth, and in Humanity) (4: The Signs of Allah for those who are certain) (6: The Signs of Allah are shown by recitation) Rejection and its consequences (7: Woe to every sinful liar) (8: The fate of those who hear the signs but are arrogant) (9-10: The fate of those who mock the signs) (10: Hell is the destination and there will be no helpers) Accountability and divine justice (3: The need to show gratitude to Allah) (6-8: The fate of those who do not acknowledge the signs) (10: Hell is their destination and there will be no helpers) (11: The Qur'an is guidance and a warning) (14: The disbelievers differ out of mutual envy) Divine power (11: Allah has knowledge of all things) (12: Allah makes use of the heavens and the earth for man) (13: Allah provides and controls creation) Guidance (14-15: Guidance to call and remain steadfast) (16: Those who argue with Allah will have no weight) (20: The Qur'an is true guidance) (20: Importance of trust and reliance on Allah)
