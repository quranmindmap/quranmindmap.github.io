Mindmap for Surah al-Haqqa

mindmap root((Soorat al-Qalam)) Divine Oaths and Affirmation (1-7: The oath of The Pen, and affirmation of Prophet Muhammad and the Qur'an) (1-4: The oath and the descriptions of the Messenger and the revelation) Rejection and Their Characteristics (4-7: The disbelievers view Prophet Muhammad as insane) (8-16: Disobedience and opposition to Allah and his messengers ) (10-16: Description of the evil characteristics of the people of falsehood) Divine Attributes and Warnings (17-18: The test and the punishment of the people of the garden as a warning) (31: Allah knows what He has prepared for the righteous) (44-47: Allah is all-powerful and there is nothing that He does not know) The Righteous and Their Reward (34-35: The description of the rewards of those who are righteous) (40: The righteous are to be patient and Allah knows those who are rightly guided) Divine Justice (28-32: Those who go astray are subject to Allah's anger) (44-47: The punishment for the evil doers is severe) The Qur'an as Guidance (48-52: The Qur'an is a reminder and a truth and the disbelievers still reject it)
