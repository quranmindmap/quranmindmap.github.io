Mindmap for Surah an-najm

mindmap root((Soorat an-Najm)) Divine Revelation and the Prophet (1-18: The Prophet's vision of Jibreel, and the divine nature of the Qur'an) (26: Angels' intercession is dependent on Allah's permission) The Hereafter and Accountability (15-19: The location of Paradise near the Lote-Tree of the Utmost Boundary) (26-30: The fate of those who turn away from the truth) (31-32: Allah's all-encompassing knowledge and just requital for deeds) The Rejection of the Disbelievers (19-25: The polytheists' belief in false deities and their rejection of the truth) (33-62: Descriptions of the disbelievers' rejection and their deeds) Divine Attributes (26: Allah is the only One worthy of worship) (60: Allah makes people laugh and weep, has control over life and death) (62: Allah has ultimate control and all actions must be directed to Him)
