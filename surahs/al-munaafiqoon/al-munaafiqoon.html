Mindmap for Surah al-munafiqoon

mindmap root((Soorat al-Munafiqoon)) The Hypocrites' False Testimony and Actions (1: They falsely testify that Muhammad is the Messenger of Allah) (1: They use their oaths as a shield against criticism) (4: They have an impressive outward appearance and eloquence but they are empty) Their True Nature and Fate (1-3: They are liars who combine faith and disbelief) (4: They are like propped-up wood) (4-6: They are the enemy and are misguided) (5: They are arrogant and turn away from being told the truth) (6: They will never be forgiven) The Believers' Conduct (4-5: Believers should avoid listening to the hypocrisy of the disbelievers) (11: The Believers should not forget that Allah has knowledge of their deeds) The Futility of Hypocrisy and Deceit (7-8: The evil of seeking to harm the Messenger and plotting against him) (9-11: No benefit will come from their behaviour) (10: They will not be forgiven)
