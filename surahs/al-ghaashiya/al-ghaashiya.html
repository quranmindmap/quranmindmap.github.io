Mindmap for Surah Al-Ghaashiya

mindmap root((Soorat al-Ghashiyah)) The Overwhelming Event and Its Impact (1: Has there come to you the story of the overwhelming event?) The Different Outcomes on the Day of Judgement (2-7: Some faces will be humiliated and in distress and burn in hell) (8-16: Some faces will be joyful and blessed in paradise) The Nature of Divine Power (17-20: Allah’s Power over all of Creation) (17-20: The Signs of Allah’s Power and his creation) (21-26: The Prophet's Mission) (27-33: The severe punishment for disbelievers in hell) (34-36: The people of paradise and their rewards)
