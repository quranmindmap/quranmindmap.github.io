Mindmap for Surah at-tur

mindmap root((Soorat at-Toor)) Divine Oaths and Signs (1-6: Oaths by Mount Sinai, the Book, the Frequented House, the raised canopy, and the filled sea) (7: That the punishment of the Lord is inevitable and nothing will stop it) The Day of Judgement and Its Horrors (9-10: The heavens will be convulsed and the mountains will shift) (11: Woe to those who deny the resurrection) (12-13: They who amuse themselves with vain discourse) (14-16: They will be forcefully shoved to hell, where they will face torment) The Rewards and Traits of the Righteous (17-20: The righteous will be in gardens of bliss) (21-28: Their families and loved ones will join them, and they will have all kinds of delights) Allah's Sovereignty and Power (21-28: Allah is the possessor of all authority and power) (29: The power of Allah over creation, and His mercy to the believers) Divine Revelation (29-31: Reminding of the divine might, by which Allah controls all) (32-34: Allah's guidance of and blessings to Prophet Muhammad) (35-43: A challenge to those who try to refute the Message and Allah’s ability to do what He wills) Divine Wisdom and Mercy (38-42: The vastness of Allah's power, his perfect justice and the need for faith) (44-46: Allah is able to punish and destroy, but He is merciful and delays)
