Mindmap for Surah At-Taariq

mindmap root((Soorat at-Tariq)) Divine Oaths and Their Significance (1-4: The oath by the heavens and the night time, and all that appears at night) (5-7: Allah creates man from a humble origin, but gives him abilities) (7: Man was created from an ejected fluid coming from between the backbone and ribs) Human Accountability (4: That each soul is protected by a guardian) (5-6: Human is created and is given a path) (8-9: Allah's Power over the creation and the resurrection) (13: What will happen on the day of requital) Divine Power and Wisdom (1-4: Allah’s might and perfect wisdom is seen in His creation) (5-14: Allah’s power over all that he has created) The Quran and Truth (11-14: The need for people to recognize and believe in Allah and the Qur'an) (13: The Qur’an is the decisive word and not to be taken lightly) (13: It distinguishes between good and evil)
