Mindmap for Surah Al-Fath

mindmap root((Soorat al-Fath)) Divine Victory and Its Significance (1-3: The Treaty of Hudaybiyah as a clear victory) (4-6: Allah's power and control) Blessings Upon the Believers (4-6: Allah bestows reassurance) (10-17: Allah bestows favor and glad tidings upon the believers) (18-21: Allah is pleased with the believers and is preparing great reward for them) Guidance and Divine Law (23: Allah's way of dealing with disbelievers is inevitable) (24: The Messenger is to convey the message clearly) (27-28: The vision of the Prophet is true, Allah will fulfil His promise) The Characteristics of the Believers (10-17: They are truthful in their pledge to Allah and the Messenger) (21: They are steadfast and strong and put their trust in Allah) (28: They are compassionate and kind to one another, but harsh on the disbelievers) (29: They are humble and righteous in their worship and in their character) Warning and Promise (10-17: Allah's promise to reward them for their dedication and effort) (18-26: Those who are treacherous will be punished and face dire consequences) (27-29: Allah's promise that the Messenger and the believers will be victorious)
