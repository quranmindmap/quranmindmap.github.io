Mindmap for Surah al-Qamar

mindmap root((Soorat al-Qamar)) The Imminence of the Hour (1: The Hour has drawn nigh, and the moon has split) The Disbelievers' Rejection and Doom (2: The disbelievers deny the truth and consider the message to be magic) (3: They follow their desires) (4-8: Allah has sent down stories as a warning, but it has no effect) (4: The punishment of hell and divine retribution are a result of their disbelief) The Messengers (8-17: Examples of those who rejected the Messengers and were destroyed) (9: Nooh’s people) (18-22: Ad) (23-32: Thamood) (33-40: Loot’s people) (41-55: Pharaoh) Allah's Power and Warnings (41-55: Allah's Power over His creation and His warnings) (1-5: Allah has shown signs and the moon has split) The Reality of the Hereafter (41-55: The Day of Judgement and the fate of those who disbelieved) (53-55: The end of the disbelievers in hell)
