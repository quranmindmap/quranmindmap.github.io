Mindmap for SurahAsh-Shams

mindmap root((Soorat ash-Shams)) Divine Oaths and Their Significance (1-7: Oaths by natural phenomena, Allah’s magnificent creation) (1-10: Oaths about the human soul, and its ability to choose guidance) Human Choice and Responsibility (7-10: That Allah inspires the soul with evil and righteousness) (9-10: Those who purify themselves will succeed, and those who corrupt it will fail) Historical Example and Consequences (11-15: The story of Thamood and how they rejected their messenger, and destroyed their wealth) Divine Power and Wisdom (11-15: Allah's power to punish those who are arrogant and disobedient) (15: That Allah is all seeing and has great wisdom)
