Mindmap for Surah Al-Bayyina

mindmap root((Soorat al-Bayyinah)) The Necessity of Clear Proof (1-3: People of the Book and polytheists could not leave their ways until clear proof came to them) The Nature of the Clear Proof (2: A Messenger reciting pages of purification) (3: The pages contain writings of correctness) The Purpose of the Clear Proof (5: The purpose of this message was for people to worship Allah alone, with true faith, and to uphold prayer and zakat) Consequences of Accepting and Rejecting the Truth (6: Disbelievers will abide in the Fire) (7-8: Believers will be rewarded in the Gardens of Paradise)
