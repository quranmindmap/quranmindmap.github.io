Mindmap for Surah al-qiyaama

mindmap root((Soorat al-Qiyâmah)) Divine Oath and Human Responsibility (1-2: The oath by the Day of Resurrection and the self-reproaching soul) (3-4: Humans will be recreated from the smallest origins) (5: Humans desire to persist in their evil) (6: The disbelievers question when the Day of Resurrection will be) The Inevitability of the Day of Judgment (7-10: The events that occur in the universe on the Day of Resurrection) (11-12: There will be no refuge except in Allah) (13-15: On that day, man will be reminded of his deeds) The Divisions on the Day of Resurrection (19: The Qur'an is easy to remember) (16-19: The Qur'an must be conveyed without fear) (20: Those who love this world have a wrong destination) The Rewards and Punishments (21: Some faces will be radiant, gazing upon their Lord) (21: Some faces will be gloomy, expecting a great punishment) (26-35: How man will be made to face the truth during death and resurrection) (23-30: What prompts belief or disbelief is divine will, for Allah is the one who controls) (31-35: The need to seek forgiveness and submit to Allah) (36-40: Allah's ability to recreate man and the power of Allah over all)
