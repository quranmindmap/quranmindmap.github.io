Mindmap for Surah

mindmap root((Soorat Qaf)) The Qur'an and Divine Truth (1-4: Affirmation of the Qur'an and the response of the disbelievers to it) (1-2: The Qur'an is a glorious book) (1-2: The rejection of the Qur'an stems from wonder) (5: The disbelievers deny the truth that has come to them) The Power of Allah (4-5: Allah knows what the earth consumes and has a record of all things) (6-11: Reflection upon Allah’s Creation -heavens, earth, plants-) Resurrection and Accountability (16-18: Allah knows what whispers in the heart and Allah has appointed recorders to note everything) (19-23: The throes of death bring the truth, and every soul will come with a witness) (23-29: Hell will be the destination of those who disbelieve and do evil) The Reminder and Warnings (30-35: Those who are righteous will be granted paradise, and its descriptions) (36-37: Those who disbelieved did not benefit from earlier generations) (38: Do not fall into doubt and be heedless) (38-40: Emphasizing the need to be patient and glorify Allah) (41-45: Allah’s power over everything and warning to disbelievers)
