Mindmap for Surah Al-Alaq

mindmap root((Soorat al-'Alaq)) Divine Command to Recite (1: Read in the name of your Lord Who created) (3: Recite for your Lord is the most generous) Human Creation and Divine Guidance (2: Allah created man from a clinging clot) (4-5: Allah taught man the use of the pen and taught him what he did not know) Transgression and Arrogance (6-7: Man transgresses when he sees himself as self-sufficient) Warnings and Reminders (14: Allah sees everything) (15-19: If people continue to transgress, they will be punished) (19: Prostrate and draw near to Allah)
