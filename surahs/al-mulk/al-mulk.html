Mindmap for Surah al-mulk

mindmap root((Soorat al-Mulk)) Divine Sovereignty and Power (1: Allah has power over all things) (2: Allah is the One Who controls life and death and will test you) (3-4: Allah's perfect creation of the seven heavens) (5: Allah has made the sky as a means to frighten and punish the devils) Consequences for the Disbelievers (6-11: The fate of those who reject Allah and His Messengers and their terrible state) (6: The hellfire is the ultimate destination) (7: The hellfire will be roaring and near bursting) (10: They will regret rejecting the messengers) Allah's Knowledge and Power (13-14: Allah has knowledge of all things; nothing is hidden from Him) (14: Allah controls all matters of provision) The Believers' Response (15: Seek provision by traveling through the land) (15: Recognize the signs of Allah in all that is around) (19: Recognize the might of the birds) (20-21: Recognize Allah’s power that nothing can withhold and His great attributes and kindness) (23-24: Be reminded of Allah’s power and that all things are from Him) (27-30: Be reminded of the coming judgement)
