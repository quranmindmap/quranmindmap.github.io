Mindmap for Surah Al-Humaza

mindmap root((Soorat al-Humazah)) The Condemnation of Faultfinders and Accumulators (1: Woe to every backbiter, fault-finder) (2: who accumulates wealth and keeps on counting it) False Hopes and Rejection of Accountability (3: thinking that wealth will make him live forever) (4: The disbelievers will surely be flung into the crushing fire) The Nature of the Punishment (5: How terrible is hell) (6-7: Allah’s own fire) (7: The Fire will reach into the hearts) (8-9: The fire will not let them escape)
