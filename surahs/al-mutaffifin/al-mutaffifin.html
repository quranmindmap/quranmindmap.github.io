Mindmap for Surah Al-Mutaffifin

mindmap root((Soorat al-Mutaffifeen)) The Condemnation of Those Who Give Short Measure (1-3: Woe to those who give short measure in trade, but demand it in full when they are the recipient) (1-3: The warning against giving short measure) The Importance of Believing in the Day of Judgement (4-6: The people should realize that they will be resurrected for judgement) The Record of the Wicked and the Righteous (7-9: The record of the wicked is in Sijjeen) (18-21: The record of the righteous is in Illiyoon) (18-28: Description of what awaits those in Illiyoon; the righteous) The fate of the transgressors (12-16: Description of the state of those who did wrong and the reason for their fate) (17: They will be told they did not act righteously in this world) (29-36: The evildoers used to laugh at the believers) (17-28: The people of hell were always keen to deny the truth) The Reality of the Hereafter and Divine Justice (1-36: The need to do good deeds in this world in order to achieve goodness in the hereafter)
