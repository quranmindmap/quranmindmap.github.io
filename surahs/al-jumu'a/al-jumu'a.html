Mindmap for Surah al-jumuah

mindmap root((Soorat al-Jumu'ah)) Divine Grace and Messengership (1: All in the heavens and earth glorifies Allah) (2: Allah sent an unlettered Messenger to humanity) (2-3: The Messenger recites, purifies, and teaches the Book and wisdom) The Believers (4: Allah’s grace is given to whomever He wills) (7-8: The believers should desire death, if they are truly Allah's friends) (9: The call to prayer should take precedence) (9-11: The need to remember Allah in prayer and in all affairs) The Hypocrites and Disbelievers (5: Those who do not benefit from the book are described as a donkey carrying tomes) (6-8: The Jews are told that they must wish for death if they are the chosen people of Allah) (11: Worldly distractions should not interfere with worship) Importance of Faith and Worship (2: The Messenger's tasks of recitation, purification, and teaching) (2: To act according to the guidance of the Qur'an and Sunnah) (9-11: The need to prioritize prayer on Friday and remember Allah always)
